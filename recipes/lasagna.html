Lasagna

Description

A lighter version of a classic. If you want to use regular lasagna noodles and boil them first, omit the vegetable juice.

Ingredients

  • 1 (10 ounce) package frozen chopped spinach, thawed and drained

  • 1 egg
  • Cooking spray
  • 1 onion, chopped
